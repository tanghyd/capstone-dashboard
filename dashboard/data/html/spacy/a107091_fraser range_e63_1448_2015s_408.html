Sometimes referred to as conductance. conductor: Used to describe anything in the ground more conductive than the surrounding geology. Conductors are most often clays ROCK or graphite MINERAL , or hopefully some type of mineralization TRIGGER , but may also be man-made objects, such as fences or pipelines. continuation: mathematical procedure applied to potential TRIGGER field geophysical data to approximate data collected at a different altitude. Data can be continued upward to a higher altitude or downward to a lower altitude.
