First Vertical Derivative ORG ( 1VD CARDINAL ) This algorithm mathematically estimates the rate of fall off of the magnetic and or gravity field with height to remove the broad TRIGGER magnetic/gravity field that is created with a body. Its effect is to sharpen the edges of anomalies and to suppress broad TRIGGER anomalies associated with deeper sources (Figure 12d CARDINAL ). Second Vertical Derivative ORG ( 2VD CARDINAL ) The second ORDINAL vertical derivative uses a mathematical algorithm to change the rate of fall of the either the magnetic or gravity field with height to remove the broad TRIGGER magnetic/gravity field that is created with a body. Its effect is to further sharpen the edges of anomalies and to suppress broad TRIGGER anomalies associated with deeper sources (Figure 12e).
