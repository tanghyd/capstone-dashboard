No topsoil was removed. No plastic ROCK bags were used during drilling and all rubbish was removed from the area immediately. During drilling care was be taken to spread spoil to allow vegetation to re-establish between sample piles, as small an area as possible TRIGGER was disturbed, salinity of ground water was monitored and water and fines were contained. Rehabilitation was to be carried out immediately after drilling holes were plugged and back filled in accordance with DME/EPA/ ORG AuDAX guidelines. An audit has been conducted to determine the state and progress of rehabilitation of old drill sites.
