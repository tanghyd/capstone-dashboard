The deposit was estimated by ResEval using Inverse Distance to Power 2 ORG (ID2) grade interpolation, constrained by resource outlines based on mineralisation TRIGGER envelopes prepared using a nominal 0.5/t cut-off grade and a minimum downhole length of 3m QUANTITY . The block dimensions used in the model were 5m NS QUANTITY x 10m EW x 5m QUANTITY vertical with subcells of 2.5m x 5m x 2.5m QUANTITY . The Mineral Resource ORG is reported using a high grade TRIGGER cut of 20g CARDINAL /t was used for the Horseshoe LOCATION , Cassidy PERSON and Pod FAC zones, whereas a cut of 10g CARDINAL /t was used for the Hangingwall NORP zone. The Mineral Resource ORG was largely classified as Indicated due to the reasonable continuity of the mineralised zones and the adequate drillhole spacing. However, zones defined by wider spaced drilling (>20-30m), poor continuity and therefore lower confidence were classified as Inferred.
