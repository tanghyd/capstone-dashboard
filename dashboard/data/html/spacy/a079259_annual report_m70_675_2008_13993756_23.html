Below the cemented layer, the heavy mineral assemblage is more typical of the Yoganup Formation STRAT with a greater proportion of unaltered ilmenite MINERAL with low Al content. Surface deposits ( possibly TRIGGER windblown) are found in various sections on top of the basement on the eastern side of the area. The small thin sandy deposits are possibly TRIGGER blown inland from the beach areas onto the harder and older deposits making up the basement of the Yoganup Formation STRAT and are often heavily altered and predominantly leucoxene MINERAL .
