The greenstone ROCK succession comprises tholeitic NORP volcanic rocks ROCK , fine grained clastic sediments ROCK , felsic volcanics ROCK and mafic intrusions lying between Big Bell LOCATION , Tuckanarra LOCATION and Meekatharra LOCATION . The principal structural unit is the NE ORG trending Abernethy Shear Zone GPE that occurs immediately west of the licence area. At the Hope River Prospect ORG gold MINERAL mineralization TRIGGER has been defined by drilling. The mineralization TRIGGER occurs along the N-S FAC trending Hope River Shear Zone LOC which splays off the regional Abernethy Shear Zone GPE . Gold MINERAL mineralization TRIGGER is associated with quartz MINERAL dolerite intrusions ROCK and requires further follow up.
