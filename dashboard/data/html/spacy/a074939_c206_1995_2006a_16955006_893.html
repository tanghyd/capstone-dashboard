The Northern Extension ORG and Western Wall domains lie adjacent to each other and were combined into a single domain for modelling purposes. The tabular mineralized TRIGGER body consists of a monocline GPE flexure with a dip of 300 CARDINAL to the east. The mineralisation TRIGGER is of relatively high tenor and constitutes the majority of the contained metal in the Porphyry ROCK Lode ORE_DEPOSIT . The resource contained within these two CARDINAL domains extend into the Porphyry ROCK Pit and into the underground workings. The geological domains were constrained by lithology, alteration type, veining and gold MINERAL grade.
