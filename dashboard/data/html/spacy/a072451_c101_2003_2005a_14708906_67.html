9 CARDINAL As accumulations of massive to disseminated Ni + Cu + PGE ORE_DEPOSIT sulphides MINERAL at the base of thick differentiated gabbro ROCK sills. A good example of this style occurs at the base of the Stillwater GPE gabbro ROCK lopolith in Montana GPE . The recent discovery of the Collurabi Ni-CuPGE EVENT mineralisation TRIGGER to the north of Laverton LOCATION may well belong to this style. Ultramafic ORG rocks generally have a strong magnetic signature. However, the known mapped ultramafic units in the Crescent Leases ORG are non-magnetic probably because they are sheared and retrogressed in fault zones.
