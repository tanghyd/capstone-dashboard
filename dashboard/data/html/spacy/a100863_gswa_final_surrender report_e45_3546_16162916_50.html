This report focuses on the southern part of the Canning Basin LOCATION , the development of which peaked during the Ordovician TIMESCALE to Silurian TIMESCALE period from about 490 CARDINAL to 410 million years ago (Ma). The southern portion of the Canning Basin LOCATION contains thick evaporite ROCK sediments ROCK (of Late Ordovician TIMESCALE age) of which some are possibly TRIGGER mineralised with potash.
