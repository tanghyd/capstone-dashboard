As a component of the drilling a hand held scintillometer was used to record all of the radiation measurements associated with all of the 1m QUANTITY drill samples and 4m QUANTITY composite assay samples. The results were reported as CPS ORG (cycles per second ORDINAL ). No significantly TRIGGER high scintillometer readings were reported. The results of the readings taken from the assay samples are also reported with the assay results from the program. The assay results report a co-incidence between the maximum drill assay grades for each of gold MINERAL and uranium ORE_DEPOSIT .
