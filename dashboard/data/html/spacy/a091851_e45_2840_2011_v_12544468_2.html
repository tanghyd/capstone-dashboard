The Project ORG is located on the Yule granitic complex STRAT (figure 2 CARDINAL ). This complex consists of multiple generations of granitic rocks ROCK , with a weak ENE foliation. A review of all of the available data from the area was conducted to locate previous work in the area and identify potential TRIGGER targets. After reviewing all of the available data from this tenement FMG ORG failed to identify any targets of economic potential TRIGGER . Therefore FMG ORG has decided to surrender this tenement (E45/2840).
