2.1 CARDINAL Regional Geology Yalgoo LOCATION The two CARDINAL main greenstone ROCK sequences of the Murchison LOCATION Province are both present in the Yalgoo LOCATION Greenstone Belt STRAT . These are the Luke Creek Group ORG and the Mount Farmer Group ORG . The Luke Creek Group ORG consists of laterally extensive TRIGGER (province-wide) lava plains, banded iron formations ROCK ( BIF ROCK ) and associated rocks. The Mount Farmer ORG group consists of nine CARDINAL distinct volcanic centres and one CARDINAL sedimentary basin ( Watkins PERSON and Hickman GPE , 1990 DATE ). The Luke Creek Group ORG is the older of the two CARDINAL and includes the Murrouli Basalt STRAT , the Golconda NORP , Gabanintha LOCATION and Windaning Formations STRAT .
