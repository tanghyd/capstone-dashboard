The Impurity Removal belt filters are a turn-key vendor package. For this study an allowance for three CARDINAL 147 m2 QUANTITY belt filters has been made. Filtrate ORG from the process containing valuable TRIGGER PLS is recovered and added to the Impurity Removal thickener overflow stream which is then directed to MSP Stage 1 ORG . Filtered precipitate, containing approximately 60% PERCENT solids, is collected and returned to the spent heap leach modules or sent to a residue storage area.
