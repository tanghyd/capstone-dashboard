To the south of the Sholl Shear Zone FAC , gold deposits ORE_DEPOSIT are confined to fault and shear zones within the Whundo Group STRAT . Most of these faults and shears appear to be related to the Sholl Shear ORG . Gold MINERAL mineralisation TRIGGER has also been recently recognised spatially associated with the layered mafic-ultramafic intrusives. At various locations gold MINERAL has been found along the edge of the large Mount Sholl Intrusion STRAT including Golden Reef FAC . Ni-Cu PERSON and PGE ORE_DEPOSIT mineralisation TRIGGER is commonly found in layered intrusions of which the Radio Hill ORG and the Sholl A1, B1 CARDINAL and B2 deposits are the largest and best known.
