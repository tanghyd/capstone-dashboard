The Wiluna LOCATION Greenstone Belt STRAT can be divided into two CARDINAL metamorphic domains, the Wiluna LOCATION domain in the east and the Matilda PERSON domain in the west. The major north west trending Perseverance LOCATION Fault (also known as the Erawalla Fault LOC ) separates the domains. The Wiluna LOCATION domain is a low grade TRIGGER , prehnite MINERAL - pumpellyite MINERAL facies, metamorphic terrain comprising mafic to ultramafic lavas with intercalated sedimentary units, felsic volcanics ROCK and dolerite ROCK sills overlain by a thick pile of felsic volcanics ROCK , tuffaceous sediments ROCK , and sedimentary rocks ROCK , interrupted by extrusion of a large volume of komatiitic lava. Primary igneous textures and structures are well preserved, and deformation is predominantly brittle. The Matilda PERSON domain is a medium to high grade TRIGGER , greenschist ROCK to lower amphibolite ROCK facies, metamorphic terrain with predominantly ductile deformation.
