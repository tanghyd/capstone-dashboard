Prior to the field survey, the results of the combined search of the Department of Environment and Conservation ORG ( DEC ORG ) Declared Rare and Priority Flora were examined for species recorded within the known coordinates 310 CARDINAL 12 - 310 25 S and 1190 CARDINAL 06 - 1190 19 E (GDA94). The results of this database search are listed in Appendix 2 LAW ( DEC ORG , 2006 DATE ). These significant TRIGGER flora species were examined on the Western Australian NORP Herbariums PERSON web page (WAHERB, 2007 DATE ) prior to the survey. Two CARDINAL vegetation groups, Eucalyptus woodland and Banded Ironstone ROCK Formation ( BIF ROCK ) vegetation were encountered within the survey area. These groups were represented by a total of 24 CARDINAL Families, 26 CARDINAL Genera and 41 CARDINAL Species (Appendix 4).
