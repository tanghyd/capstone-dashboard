This elongated lense shaped belt is bounded by major north-northwest trending fault/shear zones. The Archaean TIMESCALE sequence has been intensely folded. At least five CARDINAL possibly TRIGGER sinistral (anti-clockwise) fault zones of similar but slightly more northwesterly trend are interpreted within the widest part of the belt. These are believed to successively repeat the layered succession. Also two CARDINAL northerly trending sinistral faults obliquely crosscut the belt in this area.
